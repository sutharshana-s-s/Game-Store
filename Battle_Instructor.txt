WELCOME TO BATTLE SHIP !!!


    The instructions and rules to play this game are as follows:

    1. The player is supposed to guess the location of the ship located by the computer and destroy it.

    2. The player will be given an initial score of 10 points.

    3. If the guess of location is correct,then the player will be awarded 10 points.

    4. If the guess of the location is wrong and is within the battleship area,then the player will loose 1 point.

    5. If the guess of the location is wrong and is not within the battleship area,then the player will loose 2 points.

    6. The guesses are supposed to be entered as digits,else it would be considered invalid and the player will lose 1 point.

    
Hope you will enjoy the game.
GOOD LUCK!!!
