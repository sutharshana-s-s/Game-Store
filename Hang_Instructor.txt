WELCOME TO HANGMAN !!!


    The instructions and rules to play this game are as follows:

    1. The player can choose the interested topics among the given.

    2. Based on the topic words will be chosen at random and the player is asked to guess the word character by character.

    3. The player will get 5 chances for guessing and will be awarded 5 points for each correct guess and 1 point will be deducted for each wrong guess.

    4. If the character entered is contained in the word,the character is displayed in that place.

    5. As the game progresses, a segment of the gallows and of the player is added for every guessed letter not in the word.

    6. The player is advised not to use symbols,digits or more than one character while guessing.

    7. The contestant is not supposed to repeat the guesses which are already made.

    8. After the participant finds the complete word,he/she is awarded with 10 extra points.

    NOTE: The words with space in between is also considered neglecting the space.
    Eg: 'silver jubliee' is considered as 'silverjubliee'.
    
Hope you will enjoy the game.
GOOD LUCK!!!
